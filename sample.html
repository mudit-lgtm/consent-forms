Basic Interview Consent Form Sample

This is a straightforward interview consent form sample suitable for most non-research contexts including journalism, podcasts, oral histories, and student projects.

INTERVIEW CONSENT FORM Project: [Project/Story/Podcast Title] Interviewer: [Interviewer Name] Date: [Date] I, [Participant Name], agree to participate in an interview conducted by [Interviewer Name] for [project/publication/podcast]. I understand that: • The interview will be used for [describe intended use] • The interview [will/will not] be recorded • I may decline to answer any question • I may withdraw my consent at any time ☐ I consent to this interview being audio recorded. ☐ I consent to my name being used in the final publication/recording. ☐ I prefer to remain anonymous. Participant Signature: ___________________________ Date: ____________ Interviewer Signature: ___________________________ Date: ____________

Sample Consent Form for Research Interview

The following sample consent form is designed for academic research interviews, meeting IRB standards for human subjects research.

RESEARCH INTERVIEW CONSENT FORM Study Title: Understanding [Research Topic] Among [Population] Principal Investigator: [PI Name], [Institution] IRB Protocol Number: [Protocol #] Department: [Department Name] PURPOSE You are being asked to participate in a research study examining [topic]. This study aims to [explain purpose and significance]. PROCEDURES You will be asked to participate in a one-on-one interview lasting approximately [X] minutes. The interview will be audio-recorded and transcribed. You will be asked questions about [topics covered]. RISKS AND BENEFITS Risks: Participation involves minimal risk beyond those of everyday conversation. Benefits: Your responses will contribute to [research benefit]. CONFIDENTIALITY All interview recordings and transcripts will be stored on password-protected, encrypted devices. Transcripts will be anonymized; your real name will not appear in any publication unless you provide explicit written permission. VOLUNTARY PARTICIPATION Participation is entirely voluntary. You may skip any question or stop the interview at any time without penalty. QUESTIONS Contact [PI Name] at [email] or the IRB at [irb-email] with questions. CONSENT I have read this consent form and agree to participate in this research study. Participant Name (Print): ________________________ Participant Signature: ___________________________ Date: ____________ Researcher Signature: ___________________________ Date: ____________

Sample Informed Consent Form for Interview

This expanded sample follows the full informed consent requirements for qualitative and ethnographic research interviews. See our informed consent guide for more detail.

INFORMED CONSENT FORM FOR INTERVIEW PARTICIPATION Study: [Full Study Title] Researcher: [Name], [Institution/Department] Contact: [Email] | [Phone] IRB/Ethics Approval: [Reference Number] NATURE OF THE STUDY This study investigates [topic] by conducting in-depth interviews with [participant description]. Findings will be [used for / published in] [describe output]. WHAT PARTICIPATION INVOLVES • A single interview of approximately [X] minutes • Topics: [list key areas] • Setting: [location or platform, e.g., Zoom] • The interview will be audio recorded (with your permission) POTENTIAL RISKS The risks associated with participation are minimal. Some questions touch on [sensitive topics if applicable]; you are free to decline answering any question. POTENTIAL BENEFITS There are no direct personal benefits. Findings may contribute to [broader societal/academic benefit]. CONFIDENTIALITY Data will be anonymized. Pseudonyms will replace real names in all published material. Recordings will be deleted after transcription. RIGHT TO WITHDRAW You may withdraw at any time without providing a reason and without penalty. INFORMED CONSENT By signing below, I confirm I have been informed about this study, have had my questions answered, and freely agree to participate. Participant Name: ____________________ Signature: ____________________ Date: ________ Researcher Name: ____________________ Signature: ____________________ Date: ________

Interview Consent Form Example for Dissertation

Dissertation research typically requires IRB approval and a comprehensive consent form. Here's a standard example used at major universities. For more dissertation-specific templates, see our dissertation consent form page.

DISSERTATION INTERVIEW CONSENT FORM Research Project: [Dissertation Title] Doctoral Candidate: [Your Name] Degree Program: [Program], [University] Dissertation Supervisor: [Supervisor Name] Contact: [Email] | IRB Protocol: [#] Dear [Participant Name], I am a doctoral candidate at [University] completing my dissertation on [topic]. I am writing to invite you to participate in a research interview for my study. THE STUDY examines [brief description]. THE INTERVIEW will last approximately [X] minutes and will be conducted [format]. YOUR RIGHTS: Participation is voluntary. You may withdraw at any time without consequence. CONFIDENTIALITY: Your identity will be protected through anonymization. RECORDING: With your permission, the interview will be audio-recorded and transcribed. If you agree to participate, please sign below. Participant Signature: ___________________________ Date: ____________ Doctoral Candidate Signature: ____________________ Date: ____________

Example of Consent Form for Interview (Journalism)

Journalism consent forms tend to be simpler and focus on recording permissions and on-the-record vs. off-the-record status. See our journalism consent form page for full templates.

JOURNALIST INTERVIEW CONSENT FORM Publication/Organization: [News Outlet/Podcast Name] Journalist: [Journalist Name] Story/Topic: [Brief Description of Story] Date: [Date] I, [Participant Name], consent to being interviewed by [Journalist Name] for [publication/broadcast/podcast]. INTERVIEW STATUS: ☐ ON THE RECORD — My name may be used and my statements may be quoted directly. ☐ ON BACKGROUND — My name will not be used but my information may be reported. ☐ OFF THE RECORD — My statements will not be reported or attributed. RECORDING: ☐ I consent to this interview being recorded. ☐ I do not consent to recording. PUBLICATION: ☐ I consent to my quotes being published/broadcast as part of this story. ☐ I would like to review direct quotes before publication. Participant Signature: ___________________________ Date: ____________ Journalist Signature: ____________________________ Date: ____________

How to Adapt These Examples for Your Use Case

These interview consent form samples provide a solid foundation. Here's how to adapt them:

  • Replace all bracketed placeholders with your specific project details
  • Add or remove the recording consent section depending on your needs
  • For research, add your IRB protocol number and approval information
  • For minors, add a parent/guardian signature block — see student project templates
  • For online interviews, include digital signature instructions — see online interview templates
  • For GDPR compliance (EU research), add a data processing statement
