Podcast Interview Consent Form

Free podcast interview consent and guest release form. Covers recording, publishing, and distribution rights for podcast episodes. Download free PDF template.

Why Podcasters Need Interview Consent Forms

Podcast hosts interview guests and record conversations intended for public broadcast. Even if a guest "agrees verbally" to appear on your podcast, having a signed written consent form protects both the host and the guest by clearly defining the rights granted regarding recording, editing, publishing, and distribution.

Key reasons podcast interview consent forms are essential:

  • Documents explicit consent to be recorded and published
  • Defines rights to edit and excerpt the recording
  • Specifies platforms where the episode may be distributed
  • Addresses use of guest's name, likeness, and quotes in marketing
  • Provides legal protection if a guest later claims they did not consent
  • Establishes whether guests retain any rights to the recording

Consent Form to Interview on a Podcast Show

When inviting guests onto your show, send the consent form before the interview — ideally at the same time as your interview preparation questions. Many podcast hosts use digital signature platforms (DocuSign, HelloSign, or even a simple PDF signature) for convenience. Always retain a signed copy before publishing the episode.

Media Release Form for Interview Consent

A media release form is a broader document that not only covers consent to record and publish but also releases the host/producer from liability related to the content of the interview. For high-profile guests or sensitive topics, consider a more comprehensive media release form. See media interview consent forms → for full media release templates.
