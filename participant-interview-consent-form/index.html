What to Include

  • Full and clear statement of voluntary participation
  • Participant's right to decline any question
  • Right to withdraw at any time without penalty or explanation
  • Study/project title and purpose
  • Interviewer/researcher name and contact details
  • What the participant will be asked to do
  • Recording consent with option to refuse
  • Confidentiality and anonymity protections
  • How participant data will be used and stored
  • Contact for participant rights questions
  • Signature and date lines (participant and researcher)

Participant Interview Consent Form — Sample Text

PARTICIPANT INTERVIEW CONSENT FORM

Study / Project: [Title]
Researcher: [Name], [Institution]
Contact: [email] | [phone]
Date: [Date]

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

YOUR PARTICIPATION IS VOLUNTARY

You are being invited to take part in a [research study / interview project /
data collection exercise] about [topic]. Before you decide whether to
participate, please read this information carefully.

WHY YOU ARE BEING INVITED
You have been selected because [reason — e.g., you have experience with the
topic, you meet the study criteria, etc.].

WHAT WILL HAPPEN
If you agree to participate, you will be asked to take part in an interview
lasting approximately [duration]. You will be asked questions about [topic].

YOUR RIGHTS AS A PARTICIPANT
As a participant, you have the following rights:
• You do not have to take part in this study
• You can stop at any time, even mid-interview
• You can refuse to answer any question
• You can withdraw your data within [timeframe] of the interview
• You will not be penalized in any way for withdrawing

CONFIDENTIALITY
Your personal information will be kept confidential. [Describe anonymization.]
Your data will not be shared with anyone outside the research team.

CONTACT INFORMATION
If you have questions about this study, contact [researcher name] at [contact].
If you have concerns about your rights as a participant, contact [ethics contact].

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

CONSENT STATEMENT
I have read and understood this information. I voluntarily agree to participate.

Participant Signature: ______________________________ Date: ____________
Researcher Signature:  ______________________________ Date: ____________

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What rights do research participants have? ▼
Research participants have the right to: be fully informed about the study, give or withhold consent freely, ask questions before agreeing, decline any question, withdraw at any time without penalty, and have their data kept confidential.
Can a participant withdraw after signing a consent form? ▼
Yes. Signing a consent form does not obligate a participant to complete the interview. They may withdraw at any point. You should make this explicit in the form and honor all withdrawal requests immediately.
What is the difference between a participant consent form and a media release? ▼
A participant consent form focuses on the rights and protections of the individual being interviewed. A media release focuses on granting publication rights. Research interviews use consent forms; media interviews often use both.
Can I use one consent form for multiple interview sessions? ▼
For longitudinal research with multiple sessions, you can use one consent form that covers all planned sessions. However, if the research design changes significantly, re-consent should be obtained.
Do research participants need to be compensated? ▼
Compensation is not legally required but is ethically recommended for time-intensive participation. Any compensation should be disclosed on the consent form. It should be proportionate and should not coerce participation.
