What Is an Interview Consent Form?

An interview consent form is a legal document signed by a participant before an interview begins. It grants the researcher, journalist, podcaster, or institution explicit permission to conduct, record, and use the information shared during the interview. The form ensures transparency between the interviewer and the interviewee by clearly outlining the purpose of the interview, how the data will be used, and what rights the participant retains.

Consent forms are required in academic research (especially by Institutional Review Boards), journalism, UX research, podcasting, documentary filmmaking, and many other professional contexts. They protect both parties: participants understand what they are agreeing to, and researchers are protected from legal and ethical liability.

A properly written interview consent form covers the study or project purpose, duration of the interview, whether recording will take place, how data will be stored and protected, confidentiality provisions, voluntary participation rights, and a signature block for both parties.

Key Elements of an Interview Consent Form

Every effective interview consent form must include these core components:

1

Study or Project Purpose

Clearly describe the research study, podcast, journalism piece, or project — including its title, the institution or organization involved, and the goals of the interview.

2

Participant Rights & Voluntary Participation

Explicitly state that participation is entirely voluntary, that participants may withdraw at any time without penalty, and that refusal will have no negative consequences.

3

Confidentiality & Data Use

Explain how participant data will be stored, who will have access, whether responses will be anonymized or attributed, and how long data will be retained.

4

Recording & Publication Consent

If the interview will be audio or video recorded, or published in any form, this must be explicitly stated and consented to separately from general participation.

5

Signature Block

Include fields for participant's printed name, signature, date, and researcher/interviewer signature. For minors, add a parent or guardian signature line.

Free Interview Consent Form Template

Below is a professional interview consent form template you can download and customize for your specific needs.

INTERVIEW CONSENT FORM Study Title: [Insert Study/Project Title] Researcher/Interviewer: [Your Name] Institution/Organization: [Institution Name] Date: [Date] PURPOSE OF THE INTERVIEW You are being invited to participate in an interview for [describe the project or study]. The purpose of this interview is to [explain purpose]. Your participation will help [explain benefit or goal]. INTERVIEW DETAILS The interview will take approximately [duration] and will be conducted [in person / via Zoom / by phone]. With your permission, the interview will be [audio/video] recorded for transcription purposes. VOLUNTARY PARTICIPATION Your participation in this interview is completely voluntary. You may choose not to answer any question and may withdraw from the interview at any time without penalty or consequence. CONFIDENTIALITY All information you share will be kept strictly confidential. Your name will [not be used / be anonymized / be used with your permission] in any publications or reports. Data will be stored securely and retained for [time period]. RECORDING CONSENT ☐ I consent to the interview being audio recorded. ☐ I consent to the interview being video recorded. ☐ I do NOT consent to being recorded. CONTACT INFORMATION If you have questions about this research, please contact: [Researcher Name] — [Email Address] — [Phone Number] PARTICIPANT CONSENT By signing below, I confirm that I have read and understood the above information, my questions have been answered, and I voluntarily agree to participate in this interview. Participant Name (Print): ________________________________ Participant Signature: ___________________________________ Date: __________________________________________________ Researcher Signature: ____________________________________ Date: __________________________________________________

How to Create an Interview Consent Form

Creating a compliant, clear interview consent form takes just a few steps:

1

Identify Your Study Purpose

State the title, institution, and objective of your research or interview project clearly at the top of the form.

2

Describe Participant Involvement

Explain what the participant will be asked to do, how long it will take, and whether recording will occur.

3

State Confidentiality Provisions

Describe how data will be stored, who can access it, whether responses are anonymized, and retention duration.

4

Include Voluntary Participation Statement

Confirm participation is voluntary and participants may withdraw at any time without consequence.

5

Add Signature Block & Obtain Signatures

Include fields for participant name, signature, date, and researcher signature before the interview begins.
