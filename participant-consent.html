Participant Consent Form for Interview

A complete participant consent form for interview participation. Covers participant rights, confidentiality, voluntary participation, and data protection.

What Is a Participant Consent Form for an Interview?

A participant consent form for an interview is a document signed by the person being interviewed (the participant) confirming they understand and agree to the terms of the interview. It outlines their rights, how their data will be used, and that their participation is voluntary.

The term "participant consent form" is commonly used in academic research contexts to distinguish it from interview consent forms used in journalism or media, which may instead be called release forms or interview permission forms.

Core Participant Rights in Interview Consent Forms

  • Right to informed decision-making — Participants must receive all relevant information before consenting
  • Right to refuse or withdraw — Participation is voluntary at all times, including during the interview
  • Right to confidentiality — Participants can expect their identity to be protected unless they explicitly consent to identification
  • Right to review data — In many research contexts, participants can request to review and correct their interview transcript
  • Right to limit use — Participants can specify how their responses may be used and restrict certain uses
  • Right to ask questions — Participants must have access to researcher contact information for any questions

Participant Consent Form Template

PARTICIPANT CONSENT FORM FOR INTERVIEW Study/Project Title: __________________________________________ Researcher/Interviewer: ______________________________________ Institution/Organization: ______________________________________ Date: _______________________________________________________ Dear Participant, You are invited to participate in an interview for [study/project title]. Please read this form carefully. If you have questions, please ask the researcher before signing. YOUR RIGHTS AS A PARTICIPANT • Your participation is completely voluntary. • You may decline to answer any question at any time. • You may stop the interview at any time without explanation or penalty. • You may request that your data be withdrawn after the interview (by [date]). • Your identity will be kept confidential (or identified, as specified below). HOW YOUR DATA WILL BE USED Your interview responses will be used for: [ ] Academic research / thesis / dissertation [ ] Journalism / news publication [ ] Podcast / broadcast media [ ] Educational content [ ] Other: _________________________ Your responses will be attributed: ☐ Anonymously (pseudonym used) ☐ Under your real name RECORDING ☐ I consent to this interview being audio recorded. ☐ I consent to this interview being video recorded. ☐ I do NOT consent to recording. CONTACT INFORMATION For questions about this study: [Researcher Name], [Email], [Phone] CONSENT I have read and understood my rights as a participant. I voluntarily agree to participate in this interview. Participant Name (Print): _______________________________ Participant Signature: __________________________________ Date: ___________ Researcher Signature: ___________________________________ Date: ___________

Permission Consent Form for Interview

A "permission consent form" is another term for a participant consent form, particularly in contexts where the emphasis is on granting permission (e.g., for recording, publication, or distribution) rather than on the participant's rights in a research context.
