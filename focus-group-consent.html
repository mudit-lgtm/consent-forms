Focus Group Interview Consent Form

Free focus group interview consent form for market research, academic studies, and product testing. Covers recording, data use, and participant rights.

What Is a Focus Group Interview Consent Form?

A focus group interview consent form is signed by all participants before a focus group session begins. Focus groups involve a small group of participants (typically 6-12) discussing a specific topic — product feedback, political opinions, social attitudes, or marketing reactions — guided by a moderator.

Unlike individual interview consent forms, focus group consent forms must address:

  • The group setting and the fact that other participants will hear responses
  • Confidentiality within the group (participants should not disclose others' responses)
  • Recording of the entire group session, not just one individual
  • Commercial or marketing use of findings (for market research)
  • Incentive/compensation details (focus group participants are often paid)

Focus Group Interview Consent Form Template

FOCUS GROUP INTERVIEW CONSENT FORM Study/Project Title: __________________________________________ Research Organization/Client: __________________________________ Moderator Name: _____________________________________________ Session Date and Time: ________________________________________ Location/Platform: ____________________________________________ ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━ PURPOSE OF THIS FOCUS GROUP You are being invited to participate in a focus group discussion about [topic: product / service / social issue / concept]. Your responses will help [organization/client] better understand [research objective]. GROUP SETTING This focus group will include approximately [6-12] participants. The discussion will last approximately [X] hours. You will be asked to share your opinions on [topics]. Important: Other participants in the group will hear your responses. We ask all participants to keep the content of our discussion confidential — please do not share what others say outside this session. RECORDING This session will be [audio recorded / video recorded / both] with your consent. Recordings are used only for internal analysis and will not be shared publicly. ☐ I consent to audio recording of this session. ☐ I consent to video recording of this session. DATA USE AND CONFIDENTIALITY Your individual comments may be included in research reports or presentations as anonymized, aggregated findings. You will not be identified by name in any reports unless you provide explicit written permission. For market research: Findings from this focus group may be used by [Client/Organization] for [product development / marketing strategy / academic publication]. INCENTIVE As a thank-you for your participation, you will receive: [Amount / Type of compensation]. VOLUNTARY PARTICIPATION Your participation is voluntary. You may withdraw from the discussion at any time without penalty or loss of your incentive. ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━ I have read and understood this consent form and voluntarily agree to participate in this focus group. Participant Name (Print): _____________________________________ Participant Signature: ________________________________________ Date: ___________ Moderator Signature: _________________________________________ Date: ___________

Focus Group Consent Form for Market Research

Market research focus groups have specific consent requirements around commercial data use. Participants must understand that their opinions may be used to influence product development, marketing campaigns, or business decisions. The consent form should clearly state the commercial context and any confidentiality agreements required by the client.
