Frequently Asked Before Contacting

Before sending a message, check if your question is already answered below:

My download is not working. What should I do?

Try using a different browser (Chrome or Firefox work best). Make sure you have completed the required fields — the download button only activates when all required information is filled in. If the issue persists, please contact us with your browser and device details.

Is it really free? Will I be charged later?

Yes, all templates are permanently free. No hidden charges, no premium tier, no subscription. We support this free service through advertising.

Does ConsentForms store the form data I fill in?

No. All form data is processed entirely in your browser and is never sent to our servers. Your completed form exists only in the file you download. See our Privacy Policy for more details.

Can I request a consent form template for a specific institution?

Yes — we welcome template suggestions. Use the contact form above, select "Suggest a new template type", and describe the institution and form requirements. We will consider all genuine requests.
