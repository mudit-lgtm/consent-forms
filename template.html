What to Include in an Interview Consent Form Template

A complete interview consent form template must address seven core areas to be considered ethically and legally sound:

  • Study/Project Title — The full name of the research project, podcast, or journalism piece
  • Researcher/Interviewer Information — Name, institution, and contact details
  • Purpose Statement — Clear explanation of what the interview is for and how data will be used
  • Participant Procedures — What participants will be asked to do and how long it will take
  • Confidentiality & Data Storage — How information will be protected, anonymized, and stored
  • Recording Consent — Explicit checkbox or statement for audio/video recording permission
  • Voluntary Participation & Withdrawal Rights — Right to refuse or withdraw at any time
  • Signature Block — Participant and researcher name, signature, and date fields

Interview Consent Form Template for Research

For academic and scientific research, your consent form template must meet IRB (Institutional Review Board) requirements. Key additions for research consent form templates include:

  • IRB protocol number and approval date
  • Statement of anticipated study duration and participant time commitment
  • Explicit statement of foreseeable risks, even if minimal
  • Description of how data will be de-identified
  • Compensation or incentives offered (if any)
  • Name and contact of IRB for questions about participant rights
