Online Interview Consent Form

Free consent form for online and remote interviews via Zoom, Skype, Teams, or email. Covers digital signature, recording consent, and GDPR data privacy.

Consent for Remote and Online Interviews

Online interviews present unique consent challenges because the traditional in-person signing of a paper form is not possible. Researchers, journalists, and UX professionals conducting Zoom, Skype, Teams, or email interviews must adapt their consent process for the digital environment.

Options for online interview consent include:

  • Email the PDF consent form for wet signature, scan and return
  • Use a digital signature tool (DocuSign, HelloSign, Adobe Sign)
  • Embed a Google Form or Typeform with an e-signature checkbox
  • Verbally obtain and record consent at the start of the Zoom call (IRB must approve)
  • Use a platform-specific consent feature (e.g., some research platforms have built-in consent)

Online Interview Consent Form Template

ONLINE INTERVIEW CONSENT FORM Study/Project Title: __________________________________________ Researcher/Interviewer: ______________________________________ Platform: ☐ Zoom ☐ Skype ☐ Microsoft Teams ☐ Phone ☐ Other: _______ Scheduled Date/Time: _________________________________________ Anticipated Duration: _________________________________________ PURPOSE You are invited to participate in an online interview for [study/project]. The purpose is [describe purpose]. This interview will be conducted via [platform]. RECORDING With your consent, this online interview will be recorded: ☐ Video and audio (full recording) ☐ Audio only ☐ Screen share recording (if applicable) ☐ No recording — notes only Recordings will be [deleted after transcription / retained for X years / stored on encrypted server]. ELECTRONIC CONSENT By checking the box below and providing your electronic signature, you confirm that you have read and understood this consent form and voluntarily agree to participate. ☐ I have read this consent form, had the opportunity to ask questions, and agree to participate in this online interview. DIGITAL SIGNATURE Participant Name: ____________________________________________ Electronic Signature: _________________________________________ Date: ___________ (Type your full name above as your electronic signature, or use a digital signature tool) Researcher Signature: _________________________________________ Date: ___________

Email Interview Consent Form

For asynchronous email interviews (where questions and answers are exchanged via email over days or weeks), consent can be obtained via email reply or an attached digital form. Include a clear consent statement in your first email and ask the participant to reply with "I consent" or sign and return the attached form before answering any questions.

How to Do a Consent Form for Skype/Zoom Interviews

Best practice for Zoom/Skype interview consent:

  • Send the consent form via email at least 24 hours before the session
  • Ask participants to return a signed copy before the meeting
  • At the start of the Zoom call, verbally confirm consent is on record
  • If recording, announce this at the start: "I will begin recording now — you have consented to this"
  • Keep a record of the signed consent form in your research files

GDPR and Online Interview Consent

Online interviews that cross EU borders must comply with GDPR. The consent process must be freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous — and just as valid as paper consent. Electronic signatures (typed names in an email, DocuSign signatures) are legally valid consent under GDPR if they meet these criteria.
