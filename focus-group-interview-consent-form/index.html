What to Include

  • Study/project title and sponsoring organization
  • Moderator name and contact details
  • Purpose of the focus group session
  • Expected duration and number of participants
  • Group audio/video recording consent
  • Confidentiality agreement for all group members
  • Note that other participants will hear responses (group setting disclosure)
  • Data use: how group discussions will be analyzed and reported
  • Voluntary participation and right to withdraw
  • Compensation or incentive information
  • Signature lines for each participant

Focus Group Interview Consent Form — Sample Text

FOCUS GROUP INTERVIEW CONSENT FORM

Project Title: [Project Title]
Client / Organization: [Name]
Moderator: [Name], [Organization]
Date / Time: [Date] at [Time]
Location / Platform: [Location / Zoom link]
Duration: Approximately [duration]

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

ABOUT THIS FOCUS GROUP
You are invited to participate in a focus group discussion about [topic].
This session is part of [market research / academic study / product development]
conducted by [organization]. Approximately [number] participants will attend.

WHAT WILL HAPPEN
The moderator will guide a group discussion on [topic areas]. You are encouraged
to share your honest opinions. There are no right or wrong answers.

RECORDING
This focus group session will be audio and/or video recorded for analysis
purposes. Recordings are for internal research use only.

CONFIDENTIALITY — IMPORTANT
While [organization] will keep your personal information confidential, please
be aware that other group members will hear your contributions during the
session. We ask all participants to respect the confidentiality of others.

USE OF INFORMATION
Findings will be reported in aggregate form. No individual participant will
be identified in reports or presentations.

VOLUNTARY PARTICIPATION
Your participation is voluntary. You may leave the session at any time.

COMPENSATION
[You will receive [compensation] for your participation. / No compensation is offered.]

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PARTICIPANT AGREEMENT

I have read and understood the above. I voluntarily agree to participate.
I understand that others in the group will hear my responses.

Participant Signature: ______________________________ Date: ____________
Moderator Signature:   ______________________________ Date: ____________

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a focus group interview consent form? ▼
A focus group interview consent form is signed by each participant before a group discussion. It covers recording permissions, confidentiality arrangements, how data will be used, and each participant's right to withdraw.
Do all focus group participants need to sign separately? ▼
Yes. Each individual participant should sign their own consent form. This creates a documented record that every attendee gave informed voluntary consent.
What special considerations apply to focus group confidentiality? ▼
Unlike individual interviews, focus groups involve multiple participants who hear each other's responses. Consent forms should explicitly acknowledge this and include a request for mutual confidentiality among participants.
Can focus group consent forms be completed online? ▼
Yes. Digital consent forms can be sent via email or completed through a survey platform before the session. Electronic signatures are generally accepted for research and market research purposes.
