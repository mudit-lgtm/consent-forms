Video Interview Consent Form

Download a free consent form for video interviews. Covers recording permission, distribution rights, educational use, and image rights for recorded video interviews.

Why Video Interviews Need a Special Consent Form

Video interviews raise additional consent considerations beyond standard audio interviews. When you record someone's likeness on video, you capture their image, voice, and potentially identifiable background details. Consent must cover not just participation in the interview, but also recording, distribution, publication, and how long the video may be used.

Video interview consent is required for: Zoom research interviews, documentary interviews, educational video content, news/broadcast journalism, podcast video recordings, UX research sessions, and any other recorded video-based interview.

Video Interview Consent Form Template

VIDEO INTERVIEW CONSENT FORM Project/Program Title: ________________________________________ Interviewer/Producer/Researcher: _______________________________ Organization/Institution: ______________________________________ Date of Interview: ____________________________________________ ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━ CONSENT TO PARTICIPATE IN VIDEO INTERVIEW I, [Participant Full Name], voluntarily agree to participate in a video-recorded interview for [Project/Program Title]. PURPOSE OF THE INTERVIEW The purpose of this video interview is to [describe purpose: research study / documentary / educational content / podcast / news story]. The video will be used for [describe specific intended use]. RECORDING AND USE I understand and consent to the following: ☐ My interview will be recorded on video. ☐ My image and voice may appear in the final published/broadcast content. ☐ The video recording may be [edited / excerpted] for the final product. ☐ The recording will be distributed via: [check all that apply] ☐ Academic/research publication ☐ Educational use (classroom, training) ☐ Online platforms (YouTube, Vimeo, website) ☐ Broadcast television or streaming ☐ Documentary film ☐ Podcast (video/audio) IMAGE RIGHTS ☐ I consent to my face and identity being visible in published content. ☐ I prefer my face to be blurred or anonymized. ☐ I consent to my voice being used without video image. DURATION OF CONSENT This consent is valid: ☐ Indefinitely ☐ For [X] years from the date of this agreement. EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTION USE If this interview is for educational purposes at [Institution Name]: ☐ I consent to this video being used in coursework, training materials, or institutional publications. ☐ I consent to students or faculty accessing this recording for educational purposes. VOLUNTARY PARTICIPATION AND WITHDRAWAL My participation is voluntary. I may withdraw from the interview at any time. I may request that the video recording or my portion of it be removed from the final product within [X days] of the interview date. CONFIDENTIALITY (if applicable) ☐ This interview is CONFIDENTIAL — my identity will be obscured in all published content. ☐ This interview is NOT confidential — my name and identity may be used. ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━ PARTICIPANT CONSENT I have read and understood this consent form and voluntarily agree to participate. Participant Name (Print): _____________________________________ Participant Signature: ________________________________________ Date: ___________ Interviewer/Producer Signature: ________________________________ Date: ___________

Consent Form for Video Recording Interview at Educational Institutions

Educational institutions have specific requirements for video recording consent, particularly when recordings will be used in classrooms, posted on learning management systems, or archived. Many universities require students and faculty to obtain signed video consent forms from interview participants. The template above includes checkboxes for educational institution-specific use cases.

For student projects that involve interviewing minors, a parent or guardian must sign the consent form. See student project consent forms →

Zoom and Remote Video Interview Consent

When conducting video interviews via Zoom, Microsoft Teams, or other platforms, the consent process can be handled via:

  • Emailing the consent form PDF for wet signature before the session
  • Using a digital signature tool (DocuSign, HelloSign) for e-signature
  • Verbally obtaining and recording consent at the start of the Zoom call (if IRB-approved)
  • Using an online form with e-signature capability
