About Us

Free Consent Forms for Everyone Who Asks Questions

ConsentForms was built to make ethical interviewing easy — for researchers, journalists, students, podcasters, UX designers, and anyone who collects information from real people.

Our Mission

We believe that informed consent should never be a barrier. Whether you are a university student conducting dissertation interviews, a journalist speaking to a source, or a UX researcher running usability tests — you deserve professional, legally sound consent forms without paying for expensive software or legal consultations.

ConsentForms provides 21 types of free, downloadable interview consent forms in both PDF and Word format. Every template is designed to meet institutional, legal, and ethical standards — including IRB requirements, GDPR guidelines, and standard journalism ethics practices.

What Makes ConsentForms Different

There are many websites that offer generic consent form templates. ConsentForms is built differently:

  • Purpose-specific forms: Every form is designed for a specific interview type — not a one-size-fits-all template that users must heavily customise.
  • IRB-compliant language: Our research forms meet Institutional Review Board standards used at major universities including Penn State, SNHU, UCL, University of Edinburgh, and UTS.
  • Fill online, download instantly: Every form includes an online form builder. Users type in their details, see a live preview, and download a completed PDF or Word document in seconds.
  • No paywalls or sign-ups: Every form on ConsentForms is permanently free. We do not charge for downloads, premium templates, or advanced features.
  • Accessible globally: Our forms are used in research contexts across the United States, United Kingdom, Australia, Canada, Ireland, South Africa, Malaysia, and many other countries.
