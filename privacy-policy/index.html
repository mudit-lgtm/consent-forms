1. Who We Are

ConsentForms (consentforms.vercel.app) is a free online platform that provides interview consent form templates for researchers, journalists, students, podcasters, UX professionals, and other users who conduct interviews as part of their professional or academic work.

When this Privacy Policy refers to "we", "us", or "our", it refers to ConsentForms and the team responsible for operating this website. For privacy-related queries, please contact us via our contact page.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Voluntarily

We collect information that you voluntarily submit to us, including:

  • Contact form submissions: If you contact us via our contact form, we collect your name, email address, and the content of your message.
  • Feedback and suggestions: If you send us feedback about our templates or website, we may retain that communication.

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

Like most websites, we collect certain technical data automatically when you visit ConsentForms:

  • Log data: Your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and time and date of access.
  • Usage data: Information about how you navigate and interact with the site — which pages you visit, how long you spend on them, and which forms you view.
  • Device information: Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet), screen resolution, and language settings.

2.3 What We Do NOT Collect

Important: The consent form details you fill in using our online form builder (study title, participant name, researcher name, dates, etc.) are processed entirely in your browser. This data is never sent to our servers and never stored by us. Your form content belongs to you.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To operate and improve the website: Understanding how users navigate ConsentForms helps us improve the user experience, fix errors, and develop new features.
  • To respond to your messages: If you contact us, we use your email address to reply to your query.
  • To monitor website performance: Log data helps us diagnose technical issues, prevent abuse, and ensure the site runs reliably.
  • To display relevant advertising: We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. See the Cookies section below for more details.
  • To comply with legal obligations: Where required by law, we may process or retain certain data to meet our legal obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for their marketing purposes.

4. Cookies and Tracking

ConsentForms uses cookies — small text files stored in your browser — to enable certain website functions and to understand how visitors use the site.

4.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: Necessary for the website to function. These cannot be disabled. They include cookies that remember your dark/light mode preference.
  • Analytics cookies: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymised data about site traffic and usage patterns. This helps us understand which pages are most useful and how to improve the site.
  • Advertising cookies: Google AdSense places cookies on your device to display personalised advertisements. These cookies track your browsing behaviour across websites to serve ads that are relevant to your interests.

4.2 Google Advertising & Analytics

We use Google Analytics and Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and other tracking technologies to collect information about your use of ConsentForms and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies allows it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to ConsentForms and other sites on the internet.

You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4.3 Managing Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of ConsentForms and other websites you visit. For guidance on how to manage cookies in your browser, visit aboutcookies.org.

5. Third-Party Services

ConsentForms uses the following third-party services that may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Vercel: Our website is hosted on Vercel's infrastructure. Vercel may process access logs and technical data as part of hosting services.
  • Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics 4 to analyse site traffic.
  • Google AdSense: We display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to personalise ads.
  • Google Fonts: Our website loads fonts from Google Fonts.

6. Form Data You Enter

When you use our online form builder to fill in a consent form (entering details such as study title, participant name, researcher name, institution, date, and other fields), this information is processed entirely within your web browser using JavaScript.

This data is never transmitted to our servers and is never stored, logged, or accessed by ConsentForms. When you close your browser tab or navigate away from the page, the form data is permanently erased from your device's memory. The only copy of your completed form exists in the PDF or Word file you download to your own device.

7. Data Retention

We retain different types of data for different periods:

  • Contact form messages: Retained for up to 2 years from the date of receipt, or until the matter is resolved.
  • Server log data: Automatically deleted after 90 days.
  • Analytics data: Google Analytics data is retained for 14 months by default, after which it is automatically deleted.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You can ask us to correct inaccurate personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to erasure: You can ask us to delete personal data we hold about you (subject to certain conditions).
  • Right to object: You can object to the processing of your personal data for certain purposes.
  • Right to data portability: You can request a machine-readable copy of your data.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via our contact page. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

ConsentForms is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will take steps to remove that information from our systems.

Note that ConsentForms does offer consent form templates for interviewing minors (such as student interview consent forms). These templates are designed for use by researchers and educators who obtain separate parental consent before interviewing minors, in compliance with applicable research ethics standards.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or the addition of new features. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of ConsentForms after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
